About Us

Cooking Since October 2014

Lola's is committed to provide made from scratch Mexican food. The restaurant offers a unique and authentic experience built around original recipes that demand the freshest, hand-picked ingredients. Our mission is to show the greatness &Mexico; through service a. warmth of our race, and the essence of our food.

The combination of Mexican cooking expertise with fresh ingredients created a new style of fare that is far from what you find elsewhere, Fresh ingredients are the foundation of the made from scratch food that is found every day at Lolas's.

That unique.. is paired with unparalleled customer service and a welcoming atmosphere to create an experience that will be remembered for its warm memories and fantastic taste.
